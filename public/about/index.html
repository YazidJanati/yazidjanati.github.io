Yazid Janati El Idrissi
About

I am a second year PhD candidate in statistics at Télécom SudParis - Institut Polytechnique de Paris, advised by Sylvain Le Corff (LPSM, Sorbonne Université) and Yohan Petetin (CITI, Télécom SudParis). Previously I completed an engineering degree in applied Mathematics at Télécom SudParis.

I am mainly interested in Monte Carlo methods (importance sampling, sequential monte carlo, MCMC), the theory behind and their synergy with variational inference. These days i am mostly working on finding efficient proposals for importance sampling through the minimization of divergence measures. I am also interested in Bayesian methods and Information Theory.

Don’t hesitate to get in touch if you want to collaborate !
